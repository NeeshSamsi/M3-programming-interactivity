FAQs

What is the average cost of a hotel room?

The average cost of a hotel room can vary widely depending on the location, amenities, and time of year. In general, you can expect to pay anywhere from $50 to $200 or more per night for a standard room in a mid-range hotel. Luxury hotels and resorts can cost even more.

How far in advance should I book my trip?

For the best rates and availability, we recommend booking at least 3-6 months in advance for international trips and 1-3 months for domestic travel. During peak seasons or for popular destinations, you may want to book even earlier. Last-minute deals are sometimes available but selection will be more limited.

Do I need travel insurance?

While not mandatory, we strongly recommend travel insurance for all trips. It can protect you financially against unexpected cancellations, medical emergencies, lost baggage, and other travel disruptions. The cost is usually 4-8% of your total trip cost and provides valuable peace of mind.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), debit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. For certain destinations, we also offer payment plans where you can split the cost into multiple installments. All payments are processed through secure encrypted channels.

What happens if I need to cancel my booking?

Our cancellation policy varies depending on the type of booking and how far in advance you cancel. Generally, cancellations made 30+ days before departure are eligible for a full refund minus processing fees. Cancellations within 30 days may be subject to partial refunds or credit for future travel. We recommend reviewing the specific policy for your booking.
